About

I am an Operations Research Scientist at Lyric, where I work on large-scale optimization models for solving real-world decision-making problems in logistics, planning, and resource allocation.

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Chennai Mathematical Institute, where I was advised by Prof. M.K. Srivas and Prof. M. Praveen. My research interests lie broadly in Optimization, Statistics, Formal Methods, Machine Learning, and the intersections between them.

In Summer 2025, I worked with Prof. Toshiaki Aoki at JAIST on the formal safety verification of autonomous vehicles. This resulted in a paper that is accepted for the ACM SAC 2026 conference in the Software Verification and Testing track.

In Summer 2024, I was a Research Intern at LiVe Lab, a joint lab between Masaryk University and Technical University of Munich under Prof. Jan Křetínský. We studied LTL synthesis to create winning policies using ML heuristics.

Previously, I worked as a Quantitative Research Intern at True Beacon, an asset management firm based in Bengaluru, where I worked on factor model research and mutual fund selection algorithms in Indian markets.
